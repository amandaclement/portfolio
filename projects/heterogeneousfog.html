title

Heterogeneous Fog

type

Computer Graphics

description

A real-time rendering of heterogeneous fog in OpenGL. The objective was to strike a balance between the uniformity of homogeneous fog, which often lacks realism, and the computational demands of volumetric fog, known for its realistic appearance.

The heterogeneous fog model augments exponential fog by integrating Perlin turbulence (the layering of Perlin noise octaves) into the equation to achieve a greater amount of shape and detail.
