title

COVID-19 Emotion Analysis

type

Data Visualization

description

An interactive data visualization illustrating the emotional impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. Each dot represents a Tweet posted on March 11th, 2020, which is the day the WHO declared the COVID-19 outbreak a global pandemic. The dots are coloured based on their emotion categorization.
